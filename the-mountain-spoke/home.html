About Our Company

When Gary retired from the Navy Seals, all he wanted was a quiet life. That's when he discovered Bozeman. It's the perfect combination of fresh air, mountains, and adventure. He started the Mountain Spoke to feed his passion for service and fun. After 17 years, The Mountain Spoke has become the heart of our community. Whether it's a breathtaking ride with the family, or a romantic ride with that special someone, The Mountain Spoke has what you need.

Come Ride With Us

The Mountain Spoke offers guided tours every day of the season. Come join one of our very skilled tour guides through the beautiful Montana mountains. The best part? Skill level doesn't matter! We have a variety of different rides, ranging in difficulty from beginner to expert level. See you there!
